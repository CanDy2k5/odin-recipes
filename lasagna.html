Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • meat;
  • onion&garlic;
  • tomato sauce;
  • lasagna noodles;
  • eggs;
  • cheeses:parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese;
  • two tablespoons of sugar;
  • spices and seasonings(fresh parsley,dried basil leaves,salt,black pepper,fennel seeds);

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
